Buffalo Mac and Cheese Recipe

About Recipe

This is my go-to mac and cheese recipe that I've found to be my favorite. Works with all different kinds of cheese and I prefer it best with Frank's Red Hot Sauce. This can come together in about 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 4 cups shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (or to taste)
  • 2 cups cooked chicken (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni noodles as per package instructions.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan on low heat.
  3. Whisk in flour and cook until smooth.
  4. Gradually stir in milk and bring to a boil for 1 minute, consistently stirring.
  5. Add cheese and hot sauce until melted and smooth.
  6. Add macaroni and chicken (if applicable), and stir to mix.

Optional: Add more hot sauce on top.

Chicken is entirely optional for this recipe. I typically use rotisserie chicken when I have it.
For a yummy alternative, you could try other kinds of meat, too. For example, Twigs has Crab Mac and Cheese that could be worth a try.
